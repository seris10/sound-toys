Karplus–Strong Strings

Each string is a delay line full of noise, low-pass filtered as it loops — the simplest physical model of a plucked string. Tap a string to pluck it, drag across to strum the harp, use two fingers for chords. Where you touch sets the timbre; how fast you strum sets how hard. Strings glow in their own colour, mapped to pitch.

Tap a string · drag to strum · two fingers for a chord
